About MarketSpace

What is MarketSpace?

MarketSpace is a next-generation e-commerce platform designed to bridge the gap between local vendors and digital customers. Unlike traditional online stores, we focus on interaction and transparency. We provide the infrastructure for anyone to set up a digital storefront in seconds, while giving customers a unified place to discover unique products.

Key Features

Direct Negotiation Chat directly with sellers to ask questions or negotiate prices before buying.
Real-Time Tracking Visual order timeline updates you from "Processing" to "Delivered".
Business Tools Vendors get automatic receipt generators, inventory management, and analytics.
Instant Storefront Get a unique URL (e.g., marketspace.com/?store=mybrand) instantly.

How to Get Started

For Vendors

  1. Click "Open Store" in the menu.
  2. Fill in your Store Name, Category, and create a unique URL slug.
  3. Wait for Admin Approval (usually within 24 hours).
  4. Log in to your Vendor Dashboard.
  5. Add products, upload images, and start sharing your Store URL!

For Customers

  1. Click "Sign In" and choose "Customer".
  2. Browse "All Stores" or search for specific products.
  3. Add items to your cart or use "Chat" to talk to the seller.
  4. Checkout securely using our platform.
  5. Track your delivery in the "My Orders" tab.
